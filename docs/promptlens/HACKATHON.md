Maps to marking criteria in [`REQUIREMENTS.md`](./REQUIREMENTS.md).

## 30-second pitch

> AI answers are only as good as the ask. **PromptLens** reads your past prompts, finds your bad habits, scores every prompt from 0–100, and lets you toggle a cheap small model to rewrite your ask *before* you hit the expensive model.

## 3-minute script

1. **Problem (20s)** — Show a vague, polite, bloated prompt.  
2. **History (40s)** — Import → “Your top inefficiency: vague filler + politeness tax.”  
3. **Score (30s)** — Paste weak prompt → **42/100** with reasons.  
4. **Improve (50s)** — Toggle ON → **78/100**; show changelog.  
5. **Send (20s)** — Prepare & send; show metrics.  
6. **Close (20s)** — Cheap models for coaching; frontier model only for the final answer.
